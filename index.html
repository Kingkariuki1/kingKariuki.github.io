About King Kariuki

King Kariuki is a Kenyan creative entrepreneur, digital consultant, and founder of Evarlic Kenya. Known for discipline, integrity, and digital precision, he helps brands and individuals access reliable government services and elevate their brand identity through powerful, professional design. He is also the face behind Evarlic Cyber and Evarlic Creations.

What I Do

Through Evarlic Kenya, I offer two core services: digital facilitation of government services such as NTSA, KRA, passports, and HELB through Evarlic Cyber, and strategic brand identity and graphic design through Evarlic Creations. Every client gets customized, reliable, and confidential service – always.
