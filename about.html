About King Kariuki

King Kariuki is a Kenyan creative entrepreneur and digital consultant with a passion for empowering individuals and businesses through innovative digital and creative solutions. As the founder of Evarlic Kenya, he has built a reputation for discipline, integrity, and excellence in service delivery.

With years of experience in digital facilitation and branding, King Kariuki leads two flagship ventures: Evarlic Cyber, which streamlines access to government services like NTSA, KRA, passports, and HELB, and Evarlic Creations, which delivers strategic brand identity and professional graphic design.

Mission: To provide seamless, reliable, and confidential digital and creative services that empower clients to achieve their goals.

Vision: To be Kenya’s leading provider of trusted digital solutions and creative branding, fostering growth and success for all clients.
